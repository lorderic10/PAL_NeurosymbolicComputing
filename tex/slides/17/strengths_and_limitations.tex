\begin{frame}{Strengths and limitations}
\begin{columns}[T,totalwidth=\textwidth]
\begin{column}{0.48\textwidth}
\begin{exampleblock}{Strengths}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Clear neuro-symbolic division of labor.
  \item Strong gains on math, symbolic, and algorithmic tasks.
  \item Robustness to larger numbers.
  \item Simple implementation: prompt + interpreter.
\end{itemize}
\end{exampleblock}
\end{column}
\begin{column}{0.48\textwidth}
\begin{alertblock}{Limitations / questions}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Correctness still depends on generating the right program.
  \item Execution safety and sandboxing are not the central focus.
  \item Some tasks may not map cleanly to Python programs.
  \item Evaluation is based on few-shot prompting with specific model families.
\end{itemize}
\end{alertblock}
\end{column}
\end{columns}
\end{frame}
